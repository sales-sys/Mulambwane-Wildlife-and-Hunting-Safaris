Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Mulambwane Safaris.

How do I book a safari?

You can send us an inquiry using the contact form above, email us directly, or call our bookings number. We will guide you through the process and help tailor your experience to your preferences and budget.

What is included in a safari package?

All packages include professional guide services, accommodation at our lodge, meals, and activities as specified in your chosen package. Airport transfers and specific equipment may vary by package type.

Is Mulambwane suitable for families?

Absolutely! We offer family-friendly accommodations and activities suitable for children. Our cultural experiences and wildlife viewing are educational and engaging for all ages. Please mention your family requirements when booking.

Do you cater for special dietary needs?

Yes, our kitchen can accommodate most dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, halal, and food allergies. Please let us know your specific needs when making your booking.

How do I get to Mulambwane?

We are located in Limpopo Province, South Africa. The closest airport is OR Tambo International Airport in Johannesburg. We can arrange transfers or provide detailed driving directions upon booking confirmation.

What is the best time to visit?

South Africa offers excellent safari opportunities year-round. The dry season (May-September) is ideal for game viewing, while the wet season (October-April) offers lush landscapes and bird watching. We can advise based on your interests.
